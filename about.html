About us

Introducing "Wet Dirt," where creativity meets culinary innovation. We specialize in crafting unique and delicious food made out of mud. Join us for a culinary adventure that redefines taste and texture. Wet Dirt—where mud becomes a gourmet delight!

After the war of 1812 America fell into a famine. The world needed a strong and tasty solution to purge their hunger. The Meykleiandez family created the original recipe for Müd. Müd became a staple in every household, resturant, and store leading the world into a healthy new regime. The recipe was passed down for generations, but one day the fifth generation of the Meykleiandez family fell upon hard times. They stopped producing Müd to focus on their their active lawsuit. Müd became a thing of the past and disapeared. Until in 2010 the family came togetehr and began the tedious proccess of bring Müd back to the people.
